DETECTION SUITE

Six violation classes, read in one pass.

One forward pass over any frame returns every flagged violation — with confidence, plate and location attached. Trained on 4.2M annotated frames.

NO_HELMET
No-helmet riding

Detects bare-headed riders and pillions across two-wheelers.

TRIPLE_RIDE
Triple riding

Counts occupants per vehicle and flags 3+ on a single ride.

NO_SEATBELT
No seatbelt

Reads driver and front-passenger belts through the windshield.

WRONG_SIDE
Wrong-side driving

Tracks heading vectors against lane direction to catch counterflow.

RED_LIGHT
Red-light jump

Syncs signal phase with stop-line crossing to catch jumps.

ILLEGAL_PARK
Illegal parking

Flags vehicles in no-parking zones and on yellow-line stretches.
